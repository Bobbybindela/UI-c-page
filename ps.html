  • 8 Piston Ring Deformation Due to Heat Treatment | Automotive / Manufacturing Technology
    Company Challenge:

    We are facing a critical issue with the third piston ring (3-piece oil ring), where the rail is experiencing deformation during the heat treatment process. This deformation, resulting in bending, is causing the ring to be rejected as it no longer meets the required standards for functionality and fitment. The rings are intended for use in engines that run on 85% Ethanol fuel (flex fuel) in BS-VI motorcycles and scooters. The challenge is to refine the heat treatment parameters and manufacturing process to prevent the deformation and ensure the integrity of the third piston ring rail.

    Key Issues to Address:
    Distortion of the Third Piston Ring Rail:

    The third piston ring rail is undergoing a distortion phenomenon due to the current heat treatment process, which is causing it to bend.

    Impact of Heat Treatment:

    The deformation caused by heat treatment is compromising the fitment, integrity, and functionality of the piston ring, leading to its rejection.

    Flex Fuel Compatibility:

    The piston rings are specifically designed for engines running on 85% Ethanol fuel (flex fuel) in BS-VI compliant motorcycles and scooters, adding complexity to the material and manufacturing requirements.

    Manufacturing Process Refinement:

    There is a need to modify the heat treatment parameters and refine the manufacturing process to prevent the deformation and maintain the integrity of the third piston ring rail.

    Objectives for Solution Development:
    Refine Heat Treatment Parameters:

    Analyze and modify the heat treatment process to eliminate or reduce the deformation of the piston ring rail. This could involve adjusting temperature, duration, cooling rates, or other factors that contribute to the bending during heat treatment.

    Enhance Manufacturing Processes:

    Assess and improve other aspects of the manufacturing process that could help maintain the integrity of the third piston ring rail, ensuring that it meets the required specifications for performance and durability in BS-VI engines..

    Material Considerations:

    Evaluate the material composition of the piston ring to ensure it can withstand the heat treatment process without deforming, especially considering the demands of flex fuel engines.

    Testing and Validation:

    After modifying the heat treatment process, conduct thorough testing to ensure that the new process eliminates the deformation issue and maintains the required quality standards for functionality, performance, and fitment.

    We challenge innovators to propose changes to the heat treatment parameters and provide recommendations to refine the overall manufacturing process, ensuring that the third piston ring rail maintains its integrity and meets the stringent performance standards required for flex fuel BS-VI engines.

  • 9 Secure Valet Parking System | Automotive / Security Technology
    Company Challenge:

    Ensuring the security and integrity of vehicles in valet parking services is a significant concern for car owners. The challenge is to create a foolproof system that guarantees the safety of parked vehicles, preventing unauthorized use and assuring owners that their cars will not be taken out without explicit permission. This solution needs to monitor and control vehicle usage, ensuring transparency, accountability, and security at all stages of valet service.

    Key Objectives to Address:
    Vehicle Usage Monitoring:

    Develop a comprehensive system to monitor the usage of parked vehicles by valet attendants, ensuring that the vehicles are only driven for legitimate parking purposes and not for personal or unauthorized use.

    Geo-Fencing Technology:

    Implement geo-fencing technology to create virtual boundaries around the parking area, ensuring that vehicles do not leave the designated parking zone without authorization. This will enhance control and limit the areas where the vehicles can move.

    Ignition Control Mechanism:

    Integrate a secure ignition control system that prevents the valet from starting the vehicle without proper authorization, safeguarding against unauthorized usage.

    Real-Time Surveillance:

    Install surveillance systems that provide continuous monitoring of the valet parking areas in real time. This adds an extra layer of security and helps identify any potential misuse of vehicles.

    Digital Check-In/Check-Out System:

    Develop a digital system for recording the entry and exit times of each vehicle. This provides transparency and ensures there is a clear log of when a vehicle enters and exits the parking area, contributing to better tracking and accountability.

    User Notification System:

    Implement a notification system that alerts car owners to any significant events concerning their vehicles, such as movement, ignition attempts, or if the vehicle exits the parking area, ensuring owners are always informed about the status of their car.

    Considerations for Solution Design:
    User-Friendly Interfaces:

    - Design intuitive, easy-to-navigate interfaces for both the car owners and valet attendants to ensure the system is simple and seamless to use, reducing the chances of error and improving overall user experience.

    Legal and Ethical Compliance:

    Ensure the system is fully compliant with legal and ethical standards, particularly regarding privacy and data protection. Safeguard sensitive information about car owners and vehicle usage while maintaining transparency.

    We challenge innovators to develop a secure, efficient, and transparent valet parking system that ensures the integrity of parked vehicles, with real-time monitoring, geo-fencing, and authorization control to provide car owners with peace of mind.
